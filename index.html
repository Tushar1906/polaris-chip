Harper, 29, was named the National League MVP on Thursday, becoming the first Phillies player to win the award since Ryan Howard in 2006.

Harper hit .309 with 35 home runs, 84 RBIs and a .429 on-base percentage in 2021.

Harper helped lead the Phillies to an 82-80 record and a second-place finish in the National League East.

Harper is the first player in Phillies history to win the MVP award in his second season with the team.
